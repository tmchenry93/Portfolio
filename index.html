ABOUT

Hello! I am an Artist, a Designer and a Web Developer. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a BFA in Fine Arts, concentrating in Illustration. Two years later, I went on to complete a Full-Stack Web Development and Coding Program. As an Artist, my work begins with a strong conceptual foundation. Figuring out what event, topic or object inspires the next piece is all a part of the fun. I ask questions like What external or internal problems need to be resolved? Why is a particular issue important enough to address? For work to speak to an audience, it needs to have a complete dialogue with the artist.

This is where I found a place for web design and development. The reality that Web Developers will always be learning and evolving with the technologically time keeps it interesting. As a Web Developer, I am continuously figuring out how to communicate with a client-browser, a back-end server and everything in between. This idea of communication resonated with me,artistically and technologically. From organizing notes and cleaning up code to setting up compositions and using navigational tools like focal point, I have found a sweet point to dwell for the time being. Learning with a heavy amount of exploration. This is where I find common ground and a love for both professions.
